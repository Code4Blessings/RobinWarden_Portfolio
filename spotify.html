Spotify Song Suggester App Info

Responsibilities

The Spotify Song Suggester App takes in the user's choice in songs and then suggests more songs for the user to listen to based on the user's original choices. As the Backend Engineer, I collaborated with a cross-function team and was solely responsible for building the backend server and Restful APIs using Express, MySQLite3, and Postgres.
