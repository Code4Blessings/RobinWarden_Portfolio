About Me

Let’s get acquainted — here’s a quick look at who I am and what I bring to the table.

BIO

I’m Robin Warden — a Full Stack Developer, designer, and entrepreneur passionate about building tools that solve real problems. With a blend of software engineering, design, and automation skills, I bring creative and technical ideas to life with purpose and polish.

Software Developer

I specialize in building scalable frontends with React.js and creating powerful APIs using Node.js and Express. I also work with GraphQL, Prisma, SQL/NoSQL databases, and testing tools like Jest and React Testing Library. My experience includes modern build tools, Git version control, and a strong foundation in data structures and algorithms.

Designer

As a designer, I create clean and intuitive user interfaces using tools like Adobe Photoshop, Canva, and WordPress (Elementor & Divi). I’ve designed everything from landing pages and blogs to contact forms and digital products — always with user experience in mind.

Entrepreneur

Through Code4Serenity, I’ve built and automated a self-publishing business from the ground up. With experience in accounting, client management, and over 20 years of customer-facing service, I know how to blend business goals with tech strategy to deliver real value.
